Research Areas

Driving Innovation in Next-Generation Wireless Systems

Our Research Focus

Our lab focuses on next-generation wireless technologies, combining 5G/6G systems, artificial intelligence, and edge computing to build real-world solutions for future networks.

6G Wireless Systems

Exploring ultra-low latency communication, terahertz spectrum, and intelligent network architectures for beyond-5G systems.

AI for Wireless

Applying machine learning and deep learning for signal classification, spectrum monitoring, and intelligent wireless networks.

Edge Intelligence

Deploying AI algorithms at the network edge for real-time processing, low latency, and scalable analytics.
