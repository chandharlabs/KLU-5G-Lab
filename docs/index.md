# 6G Innovation Lab

Welcome to the **6G Innovation Lab**.

The laboratory focuses on research and development in **next-generation wireless communication systems**, including **6G networks, AI-driven wireless systems, and software-defined radio platforms**.

## Research Areas

- 6G Wireless Networks
- AI for Wireless Communication
- Software Defined Radio (SDR)
- Open RAN Systems
- Edge Intelligence and MEC
- Terahertz Communications

## Testbed Infrastructure

Our lab hosts an advanced wireless research platform including:

- 5G Core Network
- SDR-based Base Stations
- Edge Computing Servers
- AI Training Infrastructure

## Platforms

- YesDR Standard
- Deep Radio SDR
- 5-Nines Radio Testbed
